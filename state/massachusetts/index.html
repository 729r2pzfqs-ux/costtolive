Massachusetts Cost of Living Overview

Massachusetts is more expensive than the national average, with an average cost index of 149 across 89 cities. The average monthly rent across Massachusetts cities is $1,755, with median household incomes averaging $107,332 per year. The cheapest city is North Adams (index 58) and the most expensive is Wellesley (index 312).

With a top income tax rate of 9.0%, Massachusetts has one of the higher tax burdens in the US. The 6.25% sales tax and 1.15% property tax rate add to the overall cost.
