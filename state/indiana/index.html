Indiana Cost of Living Overview

Indiana is significantly more affordable than the national average, with a state-wide cost index of 71. The average monthly rent across Indiana cities is $1,059, with median household incomes averaging $69,631 per year. The cheapest city is Logansport (index 43) and the most expensive is Zionsville (index 142).

Indiana has a moderate tax environment with a 3.05% top income tax rate, 7.0% sales tax, and 0.83% effective property tax rate.
