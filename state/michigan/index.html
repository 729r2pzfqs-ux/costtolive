Michigan Cost of Living Overview

Michigan is significantly more affordable than the national average, with a state-wide cost index of 77. The average monthly rent across Michigan cities is $1,148, with median household incomes averaging $72,230 per year. The cheapest city is Alpena (index 41) and the most expensive is Birmingham (index 175).

Michigan has a moderate tax environment with a 4.25% top income tax rate, 6.0% sales tax, and 1.38% effective property tax rate.
