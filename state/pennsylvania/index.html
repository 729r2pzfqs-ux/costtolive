Pennsylvania Cost of Living Overview

Pennsylvania is significantly more affordable than the national average, with a state-wide cost index of 77. The cheapest city is Johnstown (index 32) and the most expensive is Franklin Park (index 145).

Pennsylvania has a moderate tax environment with a 3.07% top income tax rate, 6.0% sales tax, and 1.49% effective property tax rate.
