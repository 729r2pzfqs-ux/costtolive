Texas Cost of Living Overview

Texas has a cost of living close to the national average, with an average index of 96 across its 264 tracked cities. The average monthly rent across Texas cities is $1,420, with median household incomes averaging $85,052 per year. The cheapest city is Roma (index 33) and the most expensive is University Park (index 349).

Texas has no state income tax, making it attractive for high earners. However, the 6.25% sales tax and 1.6% property tax rate offset some of those savings.
