New Mexico Cost of Living Overview

New Mexico is significantly more affordable than the national average, with a state-wide cost index of 67. The cheapest city is Deming (index 40) and the most expensive is Los Alamos (index 112).

New Mexico has a moderate tax environment with a 5.9% top income tax rate, 5.0% sales tax, and 0.67% effective property tax rate.
