Minnesota Cost of Living Overview

Minnesota has a cost of living close to the national average, with an average index of 99 across its 101 tracked cities. The average monthly rent across Minnesota cities is $1,346, with median household incomes averaging $93,064 per year. The cheapest city is Hibbing (index 48) and the most expensive is Victoria (index 178).

With a top income tax rate of 9.85%, Minnesota has one of the higher tax burdens in the US. The 6.875% sales tax and 1.11% property tax rate add to the overall cost.
