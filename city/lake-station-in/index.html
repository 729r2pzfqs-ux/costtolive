Cost of Living Overview

Lake Station is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 64 (where 100 equals the national median), Lake Station residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,115/month while earning a median household income of $56,306/year.

The median home value in Lake Station, IN is $124,800, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,044 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 23.8% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Indiana has a moderate income tax rate of 3.05%. Combined with a 7.0% sales tax rate and 0.83% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Lake Station.
