Cost of Living Overview

New Brighton has a cost of living close to the national average, offering a balanced mix of expenses and amenities. With a cost index of 101 (where 100 equals the national median), New Brighton residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,327/month while earning a median household income of $87,272/year.

The median home value in New Brighton, MN is $345,900, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,415 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 18.2% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Minnesota's top income tax rate of 9.85% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 6.875% sales tax rate and 1.11% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in New Brighton.
