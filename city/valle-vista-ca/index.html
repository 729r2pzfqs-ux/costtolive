Cost of Living Overview

Valle Vista is more expensive than the national average. Higher housing costs are typically the primary driver. With a cost index of 108 (where 100 equals the national median), Valle Vista residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,666/month while earning a median household income of $61,799/year.

The median home value in Valle Vista, CA is $315,400, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,439 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. At 32.4% rent-to-income ratio, residents of Valle Vista are spending more than the recommended 30% of income on housing, indicating moderate cost burden.

California's top income tax rate of 13.3% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 7.25% sales tax rate and 0.71% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Valle Vista.
