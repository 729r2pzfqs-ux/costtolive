Cost of Living Overview

Martinsville is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 46 (where 100 equals the national median), Martinsville residents can expect to pay median rent of $784/month while earning a median household income of $42,434/year.

The median home value in Martinsville, VA is $92,500, with typical monthly owner costs around $771 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 22.2% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Virginia has a moderate income tax rate of 5.75%. Combined with a 5.3% sales tax rate and 0.8% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Martinsville.
