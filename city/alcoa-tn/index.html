Cost of Living Overview

Alcoa is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 71 (where 100 equals the national median), Alcoa residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,057/month while earning a median household income of $60,049/year.

The median home value in Alcoa, TN is $232,300, with typical monthly owner costs around $893 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 21.1% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Tennessee has no state income tax, which can represent significant savings compared to high-tax states. Combined with a 7.0% sales tax rate and 0.64% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment helps keep costs manageable in Alcoa.
