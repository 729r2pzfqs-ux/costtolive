Cost of Living Overview

Allison Park offers a below-average cost of living, giving residents more purchasing power compared to many US cities. With a cost index of 93 (where 100 equals the national median), Allison Park residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,190/month while earning a median household income of $108,669/year.

The median home value in Allison Park, PA is $328,700, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,280 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 13.1% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Pennsylvania has a moderate income tax rate of 3.07%. Combined with a 6.0% sales tax rate and 1.49% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Allison Park.
