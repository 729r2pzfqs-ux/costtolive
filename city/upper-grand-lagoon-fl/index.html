Cost of Living Overview

Upper Grand Lagoon is more expensive than the national average. Higher housing costs are typically the primary driver. With a cost index of 110 (where 100 equals the national median), Upper Grand Lagoon residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,570/month while earning a median household income of $73,438/year.

The median home value in Upper Grand Lagoon, FL is $351,400, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,486 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes.
