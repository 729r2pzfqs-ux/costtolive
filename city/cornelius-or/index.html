Cost of Living Overview

Cornelius is more expensive than the national average. Higher housing costs are typically the primary driver. With a cost index of 115 (where 100 equals the national median), Cornelius residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,475/month while earning a median household income of $90,071/year.

The median home value in Cornelius, OR is $419,000, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,552 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 19.7% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Oregon's top income tax rate of 9.9% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 0% sales tax rate and 0.93% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Cornelius.
