Cost of Living Overview

Warrensville Heights is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 56 (where 100 equals the national median), Warrensville Heights residents can expect to pay median rent of $952/month while earning a median household income of $49,256/year.

The median home value in Warrensville Heights, OH is $111,200, with typical monthly owner costs around $959 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 23.2% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Ohio has a moderate income tax rate of 3.5%. Combined with a 5.75% sales tax rate and 1.53% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Warrensville Heights.
