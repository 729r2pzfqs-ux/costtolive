Cost of Living Overview

Boca Raton has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 178 (where 100 equals the national median), Boca Raton residents can expect to pay median rent of $2,334/month while earning a median household income of $102,722/year.

The median home value in Boca Raton, FL is $660,100, with typical monthly owner costs around $2,230 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. With a rent-to-income ratio of 27.3%, Boca Raton falls within the generally affordable range, though it's approaching the 30% burden threshold.

Florida has no state income tax, which can represent significant savings compared to high-tax states. Combined with a 6.0% sales tax rate and 0.86% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment helps keep costs manageable in Boca Raton.
