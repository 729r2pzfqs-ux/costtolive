Cost of Living Overview

Riverhead has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 146 (where 100 equals the national median), Riverhead residents can expect to pay median rent of $2,208/month while earning a median household income of $83,521/year.

The median home value in Riverhead, NY is $449,000, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,922 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. At 31.7% rent-to-income ratio, residents of Riverhead are spending more than the recommended 30% of income on housing, indicating moderate cost burden.

New York's top income tax rate of 10.9% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 4.0% sales tax rate and 1.62% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Riverhead.
