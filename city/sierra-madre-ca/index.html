Cost of Living Overview

Sierra Madre has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 229 (where 100 equals the national median), Sierra Madre residents can expect to pay median rent of $2,086/month while earning a median household income of $141,094/year.

The median home value in Sierra Madre, CA is $1,172,200, with typical monthly owner costs around $2,319 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 17.7% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

California's top income tax rate of 13.3% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 7.25% sales tax rate and 0.71% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Sierra Madre.
