Cost of Living Overview

Schererville offers a below-average cost of living, giving residents more purchasing power compared to many US cities. With a cost index of 90 (where 100 equals the national median), Schererville residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,224/month while earning a median household income of $86,020/year.
