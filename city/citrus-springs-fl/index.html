Cost of Living Overview

Citrus Springs is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 76 (where 100 equals the national median), Citrus Springs residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,217/month while earning a median household income of $57,477/year.

The median home value in Citrus Springs, FL is $220,300, with typical monthly owner costs around $997 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. With a rent-to-income ratio of 25.4%, Citrus Springs falls within the generally affordable range, though it's approaching the 30% burden threshold.

Florida has no state income tax, which can represent significant savings compared to high-tax states. Combined with a 6.0% sales tax rate and 0.86% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment helps keep costs manageable in Citrus Springs.
