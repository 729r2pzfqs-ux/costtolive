Cost of Living Overview

Newark has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 249 (where 100 equals the national median), Newark residents can expect to pay median rent of $2,730/month while earning a median household income of $164,909/year.

The median home value in Newark, CA is $1,094,200, with typical monthly owner costs around $2,876 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 19.9% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

California's top income tax rate of 13.3% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 7.25% sales tax rate and 0.71% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Newark.
