Cost of Living Overview

Johnston has a cost of living close to the national average, offering a balanced mix of expenses and amenities. With a cost index of 101 (where 100 equals the national median), Johnston residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,288/month while earning a median household income of $103,430/year.

The median home value in Johnston, IA is $346,200, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,495 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 14.9% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Iowa has a moderate income tax rate of 5.7%. Combined with a 6.0% sales tax rate and 1.57% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Johnston.
