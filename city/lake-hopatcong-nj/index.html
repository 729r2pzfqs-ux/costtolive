Cost of Living Overview

Lake Hopatcong has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 122 (where 100 equals the national median), Lake Hopatcong residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,599/month while earning a median household income of $111,614/year.

The median home value in Lake Hopatcong, NJ is $388,300, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,936 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 17.2% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

New Jersey's top income tax rate of 10.75% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 6.625% sales tax rate and 2.23% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Lake Hopatcong.
