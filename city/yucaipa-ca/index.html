Cost of Living Overview

Yucaipa has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 129 (where 100 equals the national median), Yucaipa residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,661/month while earning a median household income of $92,401/year.

The median home value in Yucaipa, CA is $466,000, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,745 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 21.6% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

California's top income tax rate of 13.3% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 7.25% sales tax rate and 0.71% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Yucaipa.
