Cost of Living Overview

Dunmore is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 64 (where 100 equals the national median), Dunmore residents can expect to pay median rent of $923/month while earning a median household income of $75,632/year.

The median home value in Dunmore, PA is $186,900, with typical monthly owner costs around $998 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 14.6% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Pennsylvania has a moderate income tax rate of 3.07%. Combined with a 6.0% sales tax rate and 1.49% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Dunmore.
