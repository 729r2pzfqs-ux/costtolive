Cost of Living Overview

Henderson is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 51 (where 100 equals the national median), Henderson residents can expect to pay median rent of $835/month while earning a median household income of $38,780/year.

The median home value in Henderson, NC is $116,800, with typical monthly owner costs around $817 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. With a rent-to-income ratio of 25.8%, Henderson falls within the generally affordable range, though it's approaching the 30% burden threshold.

North Carolina has a moderate income tax rate of 4.5%. Combined with a 4.75% sales tax rate and 0.8% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Henderson.
