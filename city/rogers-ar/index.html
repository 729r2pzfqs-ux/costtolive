Cost of Living Overview

Rogers offers a below-average cost of living, giving residents more purchasing power compared to many US cities. With a cost index of 86 (where 100 equals the national median), Rogers residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,182/month while earning a median household income of $82,993/year.

The median home value in Rogers, AR is $278,000, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,212 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 17.1% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Arkansas has a moderate income tax rate of 4.4%. Combined with a 6.5% sales tax rate and 0.62% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Rogers.
