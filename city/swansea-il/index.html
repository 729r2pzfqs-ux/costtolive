Cost of Living Overview

Swansea is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 80 (where 100 equals the national median), Swansea residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,178/month while earning a median household income of $84,367/year.

The median home value in Swansea, IL is $211,700, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,323 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 16.8% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Illinois has a moderate income tax rate of 4.95%. Combined with a 6.25% sales tax rate and 2.07% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Swansea.
