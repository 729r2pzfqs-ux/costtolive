Cost of Living Overview

Merritt Island has a cost of living close to the national average, offering a balanced mix of expenses and amenities. With a cost index of 103 (where 100 equals the national median), Merritt Island residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,348/month while earning a median household income of $91,020/year.

The median home value in Merritt Island, FL is $379,700, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,295 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 17.8% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Florida has no state income tax, which can represent significant savings compared to high-tax states. Combined with a 6.0% sales tax rate and 0.86% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment helps keep costs manageable in Merritt Island.
