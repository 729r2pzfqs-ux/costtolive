Cost of Living Overview

Stanford has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 310 (where 100 equals the national median), Stanford residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,925/month while earning a median household income of $70,651/year.

The median home value in Stanford, CA is $2,000,001, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,979 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. At 32.7% rent-to-income ratio, residents of Stanford are spending more than the recommended 30% of income on housing, indicating moderate cost burden.

California's top income tax rate of 13.3% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 7.25% sales tax rate and 0.71% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Stanford.
