Cost of Living Overview

Florham Park has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 216 (where 100 equals the national median), Florham Park residents can expect to pay median rent of $3,009/month while earning a median household income of $147,714/year.

The median home value in Florham Park, NJ is $722,700, with typical monthly owner costs around $2,867 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 24.4% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

New Jersey's top income tax rate of 10.75% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 6.625% sales tax rate and 2.23% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Florham Park.
