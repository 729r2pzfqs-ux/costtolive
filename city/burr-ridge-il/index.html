Cost of Living Overview

Burr Ridge has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 200 (where 100 equals the national median), Burr Ridge residents can expect to pay median rent of $3,000/month while earning a median household income of $154,508/year.

The median home value in Burr Ridge, IL is $694,800, with typical monthly owner costs around $2,135 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 23.3% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Illinois has a moderate income tax rate of 4.95%. Combined with a 6.25% sales tax rate and 2.07% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Burr Ridge.
