Cost of Living Overview

Jefferson is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 59 (where 100 equals the national median), Jefferson residents can expect to pay median rent of $766/month while earning a median household income of $67,205/year.

The median home value in Jefferson, MO is $208,000, with typical monthly owner costs around $826 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 13.7% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Missouri has a moderate income tax rate of 4.95%. Combined with a 4.225% sales tax rate and 0.93% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Jefferson.
