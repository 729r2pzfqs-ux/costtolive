Cost of Living Overview

Grafton offers a below-average cost of living, giving residents more purchasing power compared to many US cities. With a cost index of 90 (where 100 equals the national median), Grafton residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,162/month while earning a median household income of $88,634/year.

The median home value in Grafton, WI is $301,300, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,327 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 15.7% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Wisconsin has a moderate income tax rate of 7.65%. Combined with a 5.0% sales tax rate and 1.61% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Grafton.
