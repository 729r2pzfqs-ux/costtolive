Cost of Living Overview

Carpinteria has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 213 (where 100 equals the national median), Carpinteria residents can expect to pay median rent of $2,341/month while earning a median household income of $104,233/year.

The median home value in Carpinteria, CA is $965,200, with typical monthly owner costs around $2,287 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. With a rent-to-income ratio of 27.0%, Carpinteria falls within the generally affordable range, though it's approaching the 30% burden threshold.

California's top income tax rate of 13.3% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 7.25% sales tax rate and 0.71% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Carpinteria.
