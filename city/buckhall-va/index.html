Cost of Living Overview

Buckhall has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 187 (where 100 equals the national median), Buckhall residents can expect to pay median rent of $2,591/month while earning a median household income of $169,591/year.
