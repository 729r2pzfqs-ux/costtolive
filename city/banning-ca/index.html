Cost of Living Overview

Banning has a cost of living close to the national average, offering a balanced mix of expenses and amenities. With a cost index of 102 (where 100 equals the national median), Banning residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,528/month while earning a median household income of $57,699/year.

The median home value in Banning, CA is $322,900, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,285 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. At 31.8% rent-to-income ratio, residents of Banning are spending more than the recommended 30% of income on housing, indicating moderate cost burden.

California's top income tax rate of 13.3% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 7.25% sales tax rate and 0.71% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Banning.
