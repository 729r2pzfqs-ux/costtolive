Cost of Living Overview

Havelock is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 78 (where 100 equals the national median), Havelock residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,269/month while earning a median household income of $64,433/year.

The median home value in Havelock, NC is $186,800, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,219 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 23.6% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

North Carolina has a moderate income tax rate of 4.5%. Combined with a 4.75% sales tax rate and 0.8% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Havelock.
