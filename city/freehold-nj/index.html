Cost of Living Overview

Freehold has a significantly higher cost of living than most US cities, driven largely by premium housing costs. With a cost index of 130 (where 100 equals the national median), Freehold residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,818/month while earning a median household income of $82,183/year.

The median home value in Freehold, NJ is $401,900, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,923 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. With a rent-to-income ratio of 26.5%, Freehold falls within the generally affordable range, though it's approaching the 30% burden threshold.

New Jersey's top income tax rate of 10.75% is among the highest in the nation, adding to the overall cost burden. Combined with a 6.625% sales tax rate and 2.23% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Freehold.
