Cost of Living Overview

Broken Arrow is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 83 (where 100 equals the national median), Broken Arrow residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,240/month while earning a median household income of $85,220/year.

The median home value in Broken Arrow, OK is $229,300, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,283 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 17.5% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Oklahoma has a moderate income tax rate of 4.75%. Combined with a 4.5% sales tax rate and 0.9% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Broken Arrow.
