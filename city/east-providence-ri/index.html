Cost of Living Overview

East Providence has a cost of living close to the national average, offering a balanced mix of expenses and amenities. With a cost index of 96 (where 100 equals the national median), East Providence residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,262/month while earning a median household income of $79,660/year.

The median home value in East Providence, RI is $322,100, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,428 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 19.0% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Rhode Island has a moderate income tax rate of 5.99%. Combined with a 7.0% sales tax rate and 1.53% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in East Providence.
