Cost of Living Overview

McKeesport is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 43 (where 100 equals the national median), McKeesport residents can expect to pay median rent of $786/month while earning a median household income of $31,854/year.

The median home value in McKeesport, PA is $69,800, with typical monthly owner costs around $742 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. With a rent-to-income ratio of 29.6%, McKeesport falls within the generally affordable range, though it's approaching the 30% burden threshold.

Pennsylvania has a moderate income tax rate of 3.07%. Combined with a 6.0% sales tax rate and 1.49% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in McKeesport.
