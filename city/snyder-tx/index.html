Cost of Living Overview

Snyder is significantly cheaper than the national average, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious individuals and families. With a cost index of 53 (where 100 equals the national median), Snyder residents can expect to pay median rent of $929/month while earning a median household income of $54,067/year.

The median home value in Snyder, TX is $102,500, with typical monthly owner costs around $844 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 20.6% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Texas has no state income tax, which can represent significant savings compared to high-tax states. Combined with a 6.25% sales tax rate and 1.6% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment helps keep costs manageable in Snyder.
