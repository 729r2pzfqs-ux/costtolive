Cost of Living Overview

Marana is more expensive than the national average. Higher housing costs are typically the primary driver. With a cost index of 119 (where 100 equals the national median), Marana residents can expect to pay median rent of $1,702/month while earning a median household income of $108,256/year.

The median home value in Marana, AZ is $380,100, with typical monthly owner costs around $1,649 including mortgage, insurance, and taxes. The median rent-to-income ratio of 18.9% means housing is relatively affordable here — well below the 30% threshold that HUD considers cost-burdened.

Arizona has a moderate income tax rate of 2.5%. Combined with a 5.6% sales tax rate and 0.62% effective property tax rate, the overall tax environment is a factor in the total cost of living in Marana.
