Detailed Comparison

When comparing Jacksonville, FL and Indianapolis, IN, the overall cost of living indices are 92 and 74 respectively (national median = 100). Rent is $263/month cheaper in Indianapolis, saving renters about $3,156/year.

Florida has no state income tax, giving Jacksonville residents a significant tax advantage over Indiana's 3.05% rate.
