Detailed Comparison

When comparing Indianapolis, IN and Durham, NC, the overall cost of living indices are 74 and 105 respectively (national median = 100). Rent is $300/month cheaper in Indianapolis, saving renters about $3,600/year.

Indiana has a lower income tax rate, making Indianapolis slightly more tax-friendly.
