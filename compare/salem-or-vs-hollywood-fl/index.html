Detailed Comparison

When comparing Salem, OR and Hollywood, FL, the overall cost of living indices are 105 and 113 respectively (national median = 100). Rent is $232/month cheaper in Salem, saving renters about $2,784/year.

Median household income is $6,541/year higher in Salem. Home values also differ significantly — the median in Salem is $382,400 compared to $374,200 in Hollywood.

Florida has no state income tax, giving Hollywood residents a significant tax advantage over Oregon's 9.9% rate.
