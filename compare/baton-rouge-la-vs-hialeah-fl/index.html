Detailed Comparison

When comparing Baton Rouge, LA and Hialeah, FL, the overall cost of living indices are 73 and 109 respectively (national median = 100). Rent is $514/month cheaper in Baton Rouge, saving renters about $6,168/year.

Median household income is $3,135/year higher in Hialeah. Home values also differ significantly — the median in Baton Rouge is $224,500 compared to $359,200 in Hialeah.

Florida has no state income tax, giving Hialeah residents a significant tax advantage over Louisiana's 4.25% rate.
